## 📖 What is Rycen Musics?

**Rycen Musics** is a sleek, feature-packed music player that streams millions of songs directly to your Android device. It's built around one idea — music should feel *personal*, *effortless*, and *alive*. Whether you're commuting, working out, or just relaxing, Rycen Musics shapes itself around your listening habits and keeps you in the zone.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎧 Playback & Streaming
- **Stream millions of songs** — access a massive library of tracks, albums, and playlists
- **Background playback** — music keeps playing even when your screen is off or you switch apps
- **Lock screen & notification controls** — play, pause, skip without unlocking your phone
- **Instant Equalizer** — built-in EQ with multiple presets, changes take effect immediately
- **Real-time Synced Lyrics** — lyrics scroll along with your music automatically. If none are found locally, it searches online for you
- **Zero-gap transitions** — next tracks are preloaded in the background so songs switch instantly

### 🧠 Smart & Offline Features
- **Smart Wi-Fi Auto-Download** — automatically downloads your favorites and most-played tracks whenever you connect to Wi-Fi
- **Daily Mix** — a personalized shuffle of songs from your listening history to keep things fresh
- **Battery Saver Mode** — reduces background activity to squeeze out more battery life
- **Low Data / Data Saver Mode** — uses compressed streams to save your mobile data
- **Repair Downloads** — one tap to fix any broken or corrupted downloads right from Settings
- **Download up to 3 songs simultaneously** — faster playlist downloads with less lag

### 📊 Your Stats & Wrapped
- **Personal Listening Stats** — tracks your streaks, moods, completion rates, and your favourite listening times of day — entirely on your device, no account needed
- **Spotify-like Wrapped** — a beautiful animated story card carousel showcasing your musical journey. Export it anytime
- **Forgotten Favourites** — automatically surfaces songs you've loved but haven't played in 90+ days, so you can rediscover them

### 🎨 Beautiful & Expressive UI
- **Dynamic Theme Engine** — the app's color palette shifts in real-time based on the album art of the song you're listening to, creating stunning AMOLED gradients unique to every track
- **Artwork Gesture Controls:**
  - Swipe **left/right** on the cover to skip tracks
  - Swipe **up/down** on the cover to control volume
  - **Double-tap** the cover to like/unlike a song with a floating heart animation
  - Tap the **quick tools overlay** for instant access to common actions
- **Visualizer** — a customizable audio visualizer with editable color, speed, and width
- **Animated Onboarding** — a beautiful interactive guide on first launch, also replayable from Settings

### 🚗 Driving Mode
A distraction-free interface with oversized controls, large text, and a high-contrast dark layout — designed specifically for safe use while driving.

### 🌐 Sharing & Social
- **Listening Party** — share your current queue with a friend using a short code. They enter the code and you're both listening to the same thing
- **QR Code Sharing** — generate a QR card for any playlist or song and share it instantly. Scan QR codes from your camera or gallery
- **Voice Search** — search for songs by speaking
- **Hum to Search** — can't remember the name? Just hum or sing the melody and Rycen Musics will find it

### 🏠 Home Screen Widgets
Quick playback controls and your currently playing song — right on your Android home screen, no need to open the app.

### ⚙️ Settings & Control
- **Smart Cache Dashboard** — see exactly how much space your cache is using, monitor hit/miss rates, set size limits, and clear selectively while protecting your favourites
- **Quick Access Panel** — jump to your most-used settings in one tap
- **Search in History** — find songs in your play history without scrolling endlessly
- **Import large playlists** — supports up to 100 songs per import
